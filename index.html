Covid-19 Tests Conducted Per 100k People on 1st July 2021

On 1st July 2021, Penang conducted the highest number of tests per 100,000 people. Over 2,000 people per 100,000 people were tested. Testing is important to determine if one has been infected by Covid-19 or not.

In most states, though vaccination rates have increased over the past three months, it seems that the number of cases continue to rise. This situation can be attributed to the spread of the Delta variant in the community. It is said that this variant spreads easier through the air.
